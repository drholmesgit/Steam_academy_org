Our mission with your children are knowing that everything will be alright, with unlimited channels of opportunity to achieve great things.

We offer competitive rates, scholarships, incentives as well as membership rates for one on one tutor subscription. We are proud to partner with Cisco, Microsoft, IBM, and Google to make this happen.

Our Field Trip Scholarship Summer Program will be July-August. There will be 30 lucky students, 6 per grade level that will be selected for an opportunity to get hands on experience. The grant is worth $5000, which includeds an all inclusive bootcamp experience at one of our partnership locations. They will have a fun intensive 8 week training. The curriculum is based on creating AI robots, Security+, Software Web Developement, or Graphic design.
