Our Courses

At STEAM Academy, our courses and subject matter will provide our students a cohesive, interdisciplinary educational experience. As a STEAM-centric school, we will give our students the opportunity to delve into all aspects of their education, while at the same time integrating literacy and social students course work into all areas of study.

Science

As our students progress through the school, our science curriculum will help them to understand of the physical and natural world in which they live. From the earth underneath their feet to the stars above their heads, our instruction will help them to appreciate and respect all that is around them.

Technology

In an ever-changing technological landscape, it is important that our students are able to understand and utilize all forms of technology. Basic computer coding and mechanics are just some of the topics that will be introduced to our students through both virtual instruction and hands-on application.

Engineering

Our classes will introduce our students to concepts and challenges in many aspects of the engineering spectrum. Lessons in biomedical engineering will combine aspects of biology and medicine, while an introduction to computer engineering will develop basic computer coding skills.

The Arts

In the movement from STEM to STEAM, the importance of the rts cannot be understated in the development of a well-rounded student. Whether through visual arts, performing arts or culinary arts, we will lead our students in creative adventures that will stimulate the mind and extend their boundaries.

Math

Throughout all aspects of science is the basic need for math. Our unique blend of one-on-one instruction and practice exercises will help to make math an enjoyable and rewarding challenge for our students and set them up for the future.
